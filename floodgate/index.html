FLOODGATE ANALYTICS

DESCRIPTION

This serie of charts has been designed and developed for Floodgate app. The target of the work was to redesign the old analytic page of the web app in a new way. The aim was to let the final user explore all the data collected, with a simple and enjoyable user experience, but at the same time with effective visualizations that show a concrete overview of his work. All the data presented here is mock for example porpouses only.
Here some widgets:

The first chart is showing how users of different age ranges choose different categories of payment. A mouseover let the user see the actual values of the shape selected, meanwhile clicking on the coloured dots over the range, is possible to select/deselect a shape.


The second visualization had to show directly the number of hours worked and the rate per hour (signs per hour), having as well a direct preview of the trend over the previous 7 weeks.


The third chart need to show the daily amount earn for each campaign and the sum of the campaigns' amounts. The visualization is interactive in different ways: a mouse over let us see the total of a single day, while scrolling with the wheel over the path we have the possibility to see the graph changing over time. It's possible to activate/disactivate every path just by clicking on it. The day amount and the trend will be automatically recalculated.
