THE SAINT

DESCRIPTION

The Saint is a short movie I co-worked with international team of students during my Erasmus exchange program. It's thriller/drama short film with the aim to raise awareness amongthe difference between violence in real life and entertainment violence used by the media. The pitch is about a troubled young soldier who wants to live a peaceful quiet life with his girlfriend despite perils from both his inner mind and the outside world.
